Jellycat is
more than a toy

For many people, Jellycat is not a purchase — it is a companion.

These soft objects offer emotional comfort, a form of identity expression, and a quiet way to cope with uncertainty and loneliness. In an overwhelming world, they become small but powerful reminders of warmth and safety.

When someone names a toy, carries it on a trip, or reaches for it after a hard day — they are not being irrational. They are being deeply human.

How It Became
Popular

Social media transformed Jellycat from a children's toy into a global cultural phenomenon. Fans share collections, stories, and creative scenes online — turning Jellycat into a symbol of identity, companionship, and emotional expression.

Interactive in-store experiences further strengthen emotional connection by including engaging and performing elements. This immersive and creative shopping experience makes Jellycat even more irresistible and captures customers' hearts.

Why Jellycat
Works

Plush toys can act as emotional support, offering psychological comfort and a sense of security. Their softness, familiarity, and nostalgic qualities help reduce anxiety, also stimulate childhood memories and reconnect the feeling of warmth and tranquillity.

By giving each toy a name, personality, and rich expressions — as if it had been given a human nature — Jellycat enhances the sense of connection through anthropomorphic design.

🧸

Softness

Physical touch with soft textures reduces cortisol — the stress hormone — within minutes.

💭

Anthropomorphism

Names, expressions, and personality create real neurochemical bonds — dopamine, oxytocin, belonging.

🌿

Nostalgia

Soft toys reconnect us with childhood feelings of safety, simplicity, and unconditional warmth.

🧠

Psychology

Winnicott's "transitional objects" theory: comfort objects are a healthy, lifelong emotional tool.

What is
Jellycat

Founded in London in 1999, Jellycat was originally designed as a comfort toy for children. Unlike traditional plush toys, Jellycat combines soft textures with playful and unusual designs — ranging from animals to food and everyday objects.

Each character has a name, a personality, and an expression that invites connection — carefully considered companions, each designed to be held, loved, and remembered.
